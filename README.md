# Hi there! I'm Pasquale Viglianesi

### Senior web developer | 15+ years of experience

I am a web application developer with over 15 years of solid experience in the industry. Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to tackle and solve complex challenges, managing the entire lifecycle of web applications—from server provisioning and database architecture to responsive user interfaces and SEO optimization. 

I am passionate about writing clean, maintainable, and scalable code. My deep roots in traditional web development combined with modern tooling allow me to find the most efficient technical solutions to turn ideas into concrete, reliable, and successful digital products.

In my spare time, I also enjoy tinkering with electronics and automation, bridging the gap between software and hardware using low-level languages and microcontrollers.
